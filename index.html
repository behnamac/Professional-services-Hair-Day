About Us

At Hair Day, we believe in the power of transformation. Located in the heart of Madison, Wisconsin, our boutique salon specializes in custom cuts, edgy hair colors, and makeup and nail services. Co-founded by Pria and Garry, our mission is to create a warm, relaxing space where clients can feel pampered and inspired. With a team of passionate stylists, colorists, and beauty experts, Hair Day is your go-to destination for all things beauty.
